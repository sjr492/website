About Me

My fascination with technology didn't start with a line of code, but with a YouTube review. I was captivated by the passionate debates over new smartphones, and that curiosity quickly grew into a drive to understand how it all works. Now, as a Computer Science major at the University of Miami, I'm channeling that passion into learning everything from the theory behind the code to the hardware it runs on.

I find the process of putting that knowledge into practice incredibly rewarding. It's the feeling I get from helping a friend confidently choose the right laptop, or from spending hours fine-tuning a game server to create a perfect experience. It's that drive to understand a system deeply enough to make it better, and for me, that's what technology is all about.
